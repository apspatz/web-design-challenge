Introduction

Raleigh, capital of North Carolina, is the *** largest city in the state with a population of approximately ***. The Raleigh Police Department (RPD) consists of around *** of officers serving the 6 major districts shown on the map. Each district contains a diverse and varied population.

On this site, we have created visualizations of the types and rates of crime in each of Raleigh's crime districts using data collected from the RPD's NIBRS crime API.

In order to simplify the analysis, we have grouped the types of crimes into 5 primary categories:

  • Deaths
  • Assaults
  • Sexual Acts
  • Theft
  • Other Crimes
